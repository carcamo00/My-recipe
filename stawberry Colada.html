Strawberry Colada Recipe

A strawberry colada is everyone’s favorite summer cocktail. Its sweet strawberry flavor blended with creamy coconut and pineapple creates a smooth, tropical drink that’s perfect for beach days, backyard parties, and relaxing evenings with friends.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup rum
  • 3 cups frozen strawberries
  • 1½ cups cream of coconut (Coco Lopez)

Instructions

  1. Pour strawberries, rum, and cream of coconut into a blender.
  2. Blend ingredients together.
  3. Pour into glasses, garnish, and enjoy.
