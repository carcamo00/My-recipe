Creamy Ham Spaghetti Recipe

Creamy ham spaghetti is a comforting and flavorful pasta dish. The combination of Mexican crema, sautéed ham, and seasonings creates a rich and smooth sauce that perfectly coats every strand of spaghetti. It’s a simple yet delicious meal ideal for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz spaghetti
  • 1 ½ cups Mexican crema
  • 1 cup ham (cut into small pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chicken bouillon
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • Salt (as needed)
  • Queso fresco (for topping)
  • Chopped parsley (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add ham and sauté until slightly browned.
  4. Add Mexican crema, chicken bouillon, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt to taste.
  5. Pour in 1 cup of pasta cooking water and mix well.
  6. Let the sauce simmer for about 3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan. Toss until fully coated with sauce.
  8. Serve topped with crumbled queso fresco and chopped parsley.
